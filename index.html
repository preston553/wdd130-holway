Bruce Holway was raised in Pasco, Washington, one of the three Tri-Cities in Southeast Washington. He spent most of his time reading speculative fiction novels, playing RPG video games, and drawing out his wild ideas. Bruce’s formal art education includes a concentration in illustration from Brigham Young University-Idaho in Rexburg, Idaho and a certificate in video game design from Tri-Tech Skills Center in Richland, Washington. Bruce has created illustrations for video games and entertainment media, such as character designs, setting layouts, and concept art. Bruce is currently finishing his Bachelor’s Degree in Interdisciplinary Studies at BYU-Idaho. Bruce is enthusiastic about entertainment illustration, especially for video games, but he also enjoys engaging in traditional and physical art forms. Bruce also enjoys learning about medieval and renaissance history in Europe.
